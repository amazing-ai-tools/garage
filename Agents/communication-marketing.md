# Communication Marketing — garage

Use this skill for positioning, launch copy, onboarding text, and public messaging.

## Current Context
garage is provisioned but the actual product domain is not defined. Do discovery before writing serious marketing copy.

## Messaging Rules
- Say what the app does, not what it might become.
- Keep copy direct and concrete.
- Avoid fake social proof.
- Match the audience once chosen: vehicle owners, shop operators, workshop users, or internal operators are different markets.

## Deliverables
- One-line positioning.
- Landing page headline/subhead/CTA.
- Short launch post.
- Support/help copy for the BugZero widget.
